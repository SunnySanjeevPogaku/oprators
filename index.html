Operators

Types of Operators are :

  1. Arithmetic Operators: + , - , * , / , % , ++ , --
  2. Assignment Operators: += , -= , *= , /= , %= , =
  3. Comparison Operators: < , > , >= , <= , != , == , ===
  4. Logical Operators: && , || , !
  5. Conditional Operator (Ternary Operators): (condition)? True stmt: false stmt
